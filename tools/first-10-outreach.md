# Your first 10 outreach messages — ready to send

Each is written for a specific prospect type. Fill the `[brackets]` with one real
detail (their product, their post, their stack) — that single specific reference
is what gets a reply. Point everyone to **/teardown**. Goal: book the teardown.

### 1 — AI agency / dev shop (cold email)
**Subject:** the AI spend your clients don't see

> radar: most agent workloads quietly waste 15–30% on failed calls, duplicate
> prompts, and flagship models doing simple tasks. When that overruns, it's your

> where the waste is — to the dollar. 5 min to send a log sample, nothing routes
> through you. Want me to take a look?

> — [you] · whoai.ai/teardown

## How to work these
- **Send #5 (warm) first** — fastest yeses, and they sharpen your pitch.
- ~10/day, always with one real detail in the brackets.
- Every "yes" → reply with the "send me your logs" line from `go-to-market.md`.
- Run their file through `node tools/teardown.mjs` → send the report → close the $2k.
