# WHOAI Revenue Model & Business Plan

## 1. Project Understanding
**WHOAI** is an enterprise API Gateway and FinOps operational control plane designed specifically for autonomous AI agents. As organizations increasingly adopt AI, their token spend often becomes unmanageable and opaque ("shadow AI"). 

WHOAI solves this by providing:
- **Real-time Observability:** Tracking every dollar spent by AI agents.
- **Cost Control:** A "kill switch" and budget thresholds to stop runaway agents before they incur massive costs.
- **Cost Analytics:** Identifying duplicate prompts, wasted spend, and optimization opportunities.

It acts as mission-critical infrastructure to reduce enterprise AI spend by 15-30% while maintaining secure, multi-tenant operations.

---

## 2. Pricing Tiers & Monetization Strategy
WHOAI is positioned as a high-value B2B enterprise infrastructure product. It is priced based on feature capabilities and deployment models, not just seat counts.

### Tier 1: Growth / Starter
* **Price:** **$2,000 / month** ($24,000 ARR)
* **Target Audience:** Mid-market companies or departments starting to scale their internal AI agent usage.
* **Features Included:**
  * High-Performance AI Gateway Interception
  * Real-time Cost Tracking & Telemetry
  * Basic Spend Alerts
  * FinOps Executive Dashboards

### Tier 2: Enterprise Cloud
* **Price:** **$5,000 - $10,000 / month** ($60,000 - $120,000 ARR)
* **Target Audience:** Large enterprises heavily reliant on LLMs with significant monthly token burn.
* **Features Included:**
  * *Everything in Tier 1*
  * Automated Budget Controls & Hard Limits
  * Instant "Kill Switch" for runaway agents
  * Advanced Cost Anomaly Detection (e.g., detecting 400% spikes in 45 mins)
  * Organization-level RBAC & Policy Enforcement

### Tier 3: On-Premise / VPC (Self-Hosted)
* **Price:** **$25,000+ / year** (Typically negotiated upwards of $50k-$100k+ depending on scale)
* **Target Audience:** Highly regulated industries (Finance, Healthcare, Defense) requiring strict data privacy.
* **Features Included:**
  * *Everything in Tier 2*
  * Private VPC / Self-hosted Deployment
  * Enterprise SLA & Dedicated Support
  * Single Sign-On (SSO) & Custom Integrations
  * Zero-data-retention Gateway guarantees

## 4. ROI Value Proposition (The "Why Buy" Argument)
To justify a $10,000/month price tag, WHOAI must save the company at least $15,000 - $30,000/month in wasted AI spend, or prevent a catastrophic $100,000+ billing mistake from a runaway loop.

* **Savings ROI:** "We reduce your $100k/month OpenAI bill by 20% through caching, deduplication, and anomaly prevention. You save $20k/month, and our software costs $5k. Net savings: $15k/month."
* **Security ROI:** "We ensure no customer PII ever hits the public Anthropic/OpenAI endpoints via our Gateway masking, saving millions in potential compliance fines."

## 5. Expansion & Upsell Opportunities
1. **Percentage of Managed Spend:** Instead of flat SaaS fees, pivot to charging 2-3% of the total LLM spend managed through the Gateway for ultra-high-volume customers.
2. **Marketplace/Integrations:** Paid integrations into existing FinOps tools (Datadog, AppDynamics, Splunk).
3. **AI Proxy Caching:** Charging premium rates for edge-caching repeated LLM queries, which drastically speeds up latency and saves users money.
